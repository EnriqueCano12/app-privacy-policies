Support & Help

Welcome to the Lesson Tracker support page. Here you'll find answers to common questions, troubleshooting tips, and information about using the app.

Getting Started

Initial Setup

To get started with Lesson Tracker, follow these steps:

1 Create a School

Go to Settings → Schools and add your first school or institution. This helps organize your students.

2 Add Students

Navigate to the Students tab and tap the "+" button to add your first student. Fill in their name, instrument, and assign them to a school.

3 Set Up Pricing

For each student, you can set their lesson price. This can be changed over time, and the app will track price history.

4 Create Terms

Go to Settings → Terms to create academic terms or billing periods. This helps organize lessons and invoices.

5 Start Tracking Attendance

Use the Attendance tab to record which students attended their lessons. You can mark attendance for scheduled lessons or add ad-hoc lessons.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new student?

Go to the Students tab and tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Fill in the required information (name, instrument, school) and any optional details like parent name and email address.

How do I record attendance?

Navigate to the Attendance tab, select a school, then choose a student. You can mark them as present or absent for a specific date. You can also add notes and mark lessons as ad-hoc (outside the normal schedule).

How do I create an invoice?

Go to the Invoices tab and tap the "+" button. Select a student and term, then the app will automatically calculate the invoice based on attendance records and pricing. You can add additional items like books or exam fees.

Can I change a student's lesson price?

Yes! When you edit a student and change their price, the app automatically creates a price record with an effective date. This allows you to track price changes over time while maintaining accurate billing history.

How do I send an invoice via email?

Open an invoice from the Invoices tab, then tap the email button. The app will generate a PDF of the invoice and open your device's email app with the invoice attached. Make sure you have an email account configured in the iOS Mail app.

What's the difference between "Attended Only" and "All Scheduled" billing?

Attended Only: Only charges for lessons where the student was marked as present.
All Scheduled: Charges for all scheduled lessons, regardless of attendance. This is useful for fixed-term billing where students pay for the full term.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Account & Data → Export Data. The app will generate a JSON file containing all your data and open your email app so you can send it to yourself or another device.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your data will be included in your device backups. However, we recommend regularly exporting your data as an additional backup measure.

Can I use this app on multiple devices?

Currently, data is stored locally on each device. To transfer data between devices, use the Export Data feature to send yourself the JSON file, then import it on the other device (if import functionality is available in a future update).

What happens if I delete a student?

Deleting a student will also delete all associated records including attendance, invoices, price history, and schedule history. This action cannot be undone, so make sure to export your data first if you need to preserve it.

Troubleshooting

Email Not Working

Issue: Cannot send invoices or export data via email.

Solution:

  • Make sure you have at least one email account configured in the iOS Settings → Mail app
  • Check that your email account is properly set up and can send emails
  • Try sending a test email from the Mail app first to verify your account works
  • If using a work email, check with your IT department about any restrictions

PDF Generation Issues

Issue: Invoice PDFs are not generating or appear blank.

Solution:

  • Make sure the invoice has at least one item
  • Check that the student and term information is complete
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • If the problem persists, try deleting and recreating the invoice

Data Not Saving

Issue: Changes to students, attendance, or invoices are not being saved.

Solution:

  • Make sure you're tapping "Save" or "Done" after making changes
  • Check that required fields are filled in (name, instrument, school for students)
  • Verify your device has sufficient storage space
  • Try restarting the app if the issue continues

App Crashes or Freezes

Issue: The app crashes or becomes unresponsive.

Solution:

  • Close the app completely and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Check that your iOS version is up to date
  • If the problem persists, export your data and contact support

Features Guide

Student Management

  • Adding Students: Include name, instrument, school, and optional details like parent name and email
  • Student Profiles: Track lesson duration, billing mode, active status, and whether the student is a minor
  • Price History: The app automatically tracks price changes with effective dates
  • Schedule History: Track when students' lesson schedules change

Attendance Tracking

  • Marking Attendance: Record present/absent status for each lesson
  • Ad-Hoc Lessons: Mark lessons that occur outside the normal schedule
  • Notes: Add notes to attendance records for additional context
  • Quick Access: Select by school and then student for efficient recording

Invoice Management

  • Automatic Calculation: Invoices are automatically calculated based on attendance and pricing
  • Invoice Status: Track invoices as Draft, Sent, Paid, or Void
  • Multiple Items: Add lesson charges, books, exam fees, and other items
  • PDF Export: Generate professional PDF invoices for emailing

Data Management

  • Export: Export all data as JSON for backup or transfer
  • Delete: Delete individual records or all data at once
  • Statistics: View database statistics in Account & Data

Tips & Best Practices

Organizing Your Data

  • Create separate schools for different locations or teaching contexts
  • Use terms to organize billing periods (e.g., "Fall 2025", "Spring 2026")
  • Keep student email addresses updated for easy invoice delivery
  • Mark inactive students rather than deleting them to preserve history

Billing Efficiency

  • Set up student schedules when adding them to make attendance tracking faster
  • Use the correct billing mode (Attended Only vs. All Scheduled) for each student
  • Review invoices before sending to ensure accuracy
  • Update invoice status as you send and receive payments

Backup Recommendations

  • Export your data monthly or before major iOS updates
  • Keep exported JSON files in a safe location (cloud storage, email, etc.)
  • Enable iCloud Backup on your device for automatic backups
  • Before deleting large amounts of data, always export first
